Real Number Line

Definition

Let \( V \) be a vector space. Then a set of vectors \( x_1, x_2, \dots, x_n \in V \) are linearly dependent iff the set of scalars \( c_1, c_2, \dots, c_n \) not all zero and $$ \sum^n_{i=1} c_i x_i = 0$$
